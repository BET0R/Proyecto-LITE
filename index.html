WHAT IS CHERNARUS LITE?

THE CHERNARUS LITE PROJECT IS A SIMPLE MODIFICATION FOR DAYZ STANDALONE ON ITS MAIN MAP CHERNARUS THAT INVOLVES A 50% REDUCTION OF ITS TOTAL SIZE, A CUSTOM MISSION CONFIGURATION, NEW POINTS OF INTEREST AROUND THE MAP, MORE THAN 11.000 MANUALLY PACED OBJECTS TO PROVIDE WITH A MORE APOCALYPTIC FEELING AND A SERIES OF QUALITY OF LIFE ADDITIONS. IT WAS DESIGNED TO OFFER A MORE DYNAMIC, ENTERTAINING AND DIFFERENT GAMEPLAY EXPERIENCE FOR GROWING COMMUNITY SERVERS WITH POPULATIONS AROUND 30-40 PLAYERS AND WHERE THE ENTIRE CHERNARUS MAP CAN FEEL EMPTY. ALL THIS WITH PARTICULAR ATTENTION TO PERFORMANCE (CLIENT AND SERVER) AND BALANCE FOR AN EXPERIENCE AS SIMILAR AS POSSIBLE TO THE VANILLA EXPERIENCE.

PROBABLY THE MOST IMPORTANT ASPECTS OF THE LITE PROJECT IS THAT ITS GOING TO BE 100% FREE FOR EVERYONE TO USE. NO SERVER EXCLUSIVE, NO PAYWALL, NO RESTRICTIONS. ALSO, SINCE WE DIDNT USE ANY KIND OF MODDED CONTENT AND WE KNOW THAT CONSOLE SERVERS ARE URGING FOR A DIFFERENT EXPERIENCE AND AN ALTERNATIVE TO MASSIVE MAPS SUCH US CHERNARUS OR LIVONIA, THE CHERNARUS LITE PROJECT WILL BE 100% COMPATIBLE WITH CONSOLES (JSON FILES, VANILLA MISSIONS CONFIG, QOL IMPROVEMENTS, AND MORE.).

WE HOPE YOU FIND IT USEFUL AND THAT THE DAYZ COMMUNITY CAN ENJOY A DIFFERENT EXPERIENCE. WE ARE JUST A GROUP OF PLAYERS WHO LOVE THE GAME AND TRY TO GIVE BACK TO THE COMMUNITY SOMETHING INTERESTING BY PUTTING A LOT OF WORK BEHIND IT.
