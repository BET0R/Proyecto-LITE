CHERNARUS LITE

PROJECT SUMMARY

THE CHERNARUS LITE PROJECT IS A FREE MODIFICATION FOR DAYZ STANDALONE ON ITS MAIN MAP CHERNARUS, MADE BY AND FOR THE COMMUNITY. IT INVOLVES A 50% REDUCTION OF ITS TOTAL SIZE, A MORE INTERACTIVE GAMEPLAY APPROACH, A CUSTOM VANILLA MISSION CONFIGURATION, NEW POINTS OF INTEREST AROUND THE MAP, +11.000 MANUALLY PLACED OBJECTS AND A SERIES OF QUALITY-OF-LIFE IMPROVEMENTS. IT WAS CREATED TO OFFER A MORE DYNAMIC EXPERIENCE, COMPATIBLE WITH SMALLER/GROWING COMMUNITY SERVERS AND 100% COMPATIBLE WITH CONSOLES. LEARN MORE ABOUT THE LITE PROJECT ON OUR FEATURES TAB.
