F.A.Q

WHAT IS CHERNARUS LITE?

AS WE SAID, CHERNARUS LITE REPRESENTS A COMMUNITY CREATION, FROM AND FOR THE DAYZ COMMUNITY, 100% FREE AND COMPATIBLE WITH BOTH PC AND CONSOLES, CONSISTING OF A REDUCED VERSION OF THE CURRENT CHERNARUS MAP (AROUND 50% OF ITS TOTAL SIZE), IN ADDITION TO A CUSTOM MISSION CONFIGURATION, NEW POINTS OF INTEREST AROUND THE MAP, MORE THAN 11.000 MANUALLY PACED OBJECTS TO PROVIDE WITH A MORE APOCALYPTIC FEELING AND A SERIES OF QUALITY OF LIFE ADDITIONS. IT WAS DESIGNED TO OFFER A MORE DYNAMIC, ENTERTAINING AND DIFFERENT GAMEPLAY EXPERIENCE FOR GROWING COMMUNITY SERVERS WITH MID POPULATION 30-40 PLAYERS. ALL THIS WITH PARTICULAR ATTENTION TO PERFORMANCE (CLIENT AND SERVER) AND BALANCE TO ACHEVE AN EXPERIENCE AS SIMILAR AS POSSIBLE TO THE VANILLA ONE.

IS IT COMPATIBLE WITH CONSOLES?

THE LITE PROJECT IS GOING TO BE 100% FREE FOR EVERYONE TO USE. NO SERVER EXCLUSIVE, NO PAYWALL OR RESTRICTIONS. ALSO, SINCE WE DIDNT USE ANY KIND OF MODDED CONTENT AND WE KNOW THAT CONSOLE SERVERS ARE URGING FOR A DIFFERENT EXPERIENCE AND AN ALTERNATIVE TO MASSIVE MAPS SUCH US CHERNARUS OR LIVONIA, THE CHERNARUS LITE PROJECT WILL BE 100% COMPATIBLE WITH CONSOLES (JSON FILES, VANILLA MISSIONS CONFIG, QOL IMPROVEMENTS, AND MORE.).

WHAT STAGE OF DEVELOPMENT IS THE PROJECT AT

CHERNARUS LITE IS AT 95% AT THE MOMENT. WE HAVE BEEN HOSTING OUR OWN TEST SERVER FOR A MONTH NOW TO GET FEEDBACK, RECEIVE REPORTS AND ADJUST THE LAST DETAILS BUT IT IS A PROJECT THAT IS ALMOST FINISHED AND WE HOPE TO SEE IT RELEASED IN THE NEXT FEW WEEKS.

WILL I BE ABLE TO USE IT ON MY SERVER?

DEFINITELY. WHETHER YOU ARE A PC, PLAYSTATION OR XBOX SERVER OWNER, CHERNARUS LITE CAN BE USED ON YOUR SERVER 100% FREE OF CHARGE AND WITHOUT ANY PAYWALL. ONCE WE ACHIEVE OUR FIRST 1.0 BUILD, ALL THE FILES WILL BE PUBLISHED ALONG WITH SOME INSTALLATION GUIDES FOR THOSE WHO ARE NOT FAMILIAR WITH EDITING DAYZ MISSIONS AND/OR JSON FILES. HOWEVER, IT IS NOT COMPLEX TO INSTALL IF YOU ARE ALREADY FAMILIAR WITH SERVER ADMINISTRATION.
