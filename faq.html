F.A.Q

WHAT IS CHERNARUS LITE?

IS A SIMPLE MODIFICATION FOR CHERNARUS MAP THAT INVOLVES A 50% REDUCTION OF ITS TOTAL SIZE, A CUSTOM MISSION CONFIGURATION, NEW POINTS OF INTEREST AROUND THE MAP, MORE THAN 11.000 MANUALLY PACED OBJECTS TO PROVIDE WITH A MORE APOCALYPTIC FEELING AND A SERIES OF QUALITY OF LIFE ADDITIONS. ITS FREE TO USE AND 100% COMPATIBLE WITH BOTH PC AND CONSOLES.

WHAT STAGE OF DEVELOPMENT IS THE PROJECT AT

CHERNARUS LITE IS AT 95% AT THE MOMENT. WE HAVE BEEN HOSTING OUR OWN TEST SERVER FOR A MONTH NOW TO GET FEEDBACK, RECEIVE REPORTS AND ADJUST THE LAST DETAILS BUT IT IS A PROJECT THAT IS ALMOST FINISHED AND WE HOPE TO SEE IT RELEASED IN THE NEXT FEW WEEKS.

WILL I BE ABLE TO USE IT ON MY SERVER?

DEFINITELY. WHETHER YOU ARE A PC, PLAYSTATION OR XBOX SERVER OWNER, CHERNARUS LITE CAN BE USED ON YOUR SERVER 100% FREE OF CHARGE AND WITHOUT ANY PAYWALL. ONCE WE ACHIEVE OUR FIRST 1.0 BUILD, ALL THE FILES WILL BE PUBLISHED ALONG WITH SOME INSTALLATION GUIDES FOR THOSE WHO ARE NOT FAMILIAR WITH EDITING DAYZ MISSIONS AND/OR JSON FILES. HOWEVER, IT IS NOT COMPLEX TO INSTALL IF YOU ARE ALREADY FAMILIAR WITH SERVER ADMINISTRATION.
